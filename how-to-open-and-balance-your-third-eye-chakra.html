third-eye Chakra ajna

The third-eye Chakra: What is it?

The third-eye chakra is the sixth of seven major energy centers in the body. Also known as the ajna which when translated means the “the Command center”.

A balanced third-eye chakra can mean that your psychic abilities are intact. Some have the ability to leave their body at will while others have the ability to use ESP (extra sensory perception) to communicate using thoughts. There are many psychic abilities that one can obtain through balancing the third eye chakra, especially with meditation.

Everyday we are presented with opportunities to balance out our third eye chakra but it's best to be clear about why you want to do so. Intuiton is one of the greatest gifts that we as humamn beings possess. It alerts us to danger and directs us in the path of prosperity and truth.

The third-eye chakra is an important energy center that can be blocked by a variety of things such as anger, overthinking and doubt. If your third-eye chakra is blocked it can lead to feeling frustrated, sad, angry. The good news is that you have the power to unblock your third-eye chakra through self-love, meditation, healthy diet, exercise and being with those you love. Keep reading if you'd like to hear how meditation can help you to open your third eye so that you can stay woke.

Meditation and O-M Chanting to balance the third-eye Chakra

When I first opened my third eye I had been meditating and following a healthy diet for about 2-3 months. I was meditating every day from 5 minutes up to half n hour on other days. I entered into a deep trance and saw what appeared to be a lotus rotating in mid air with a bright yellow gem protruding from the middle of it. I could go into detail but I'll keep this post limited to the third eye chakra alone.

When this centre is underactive or blocked we may find it difficult to feel motivated because our aability to exercise discernment effectively is inhibited. When our intuitive ability is blocked we may find it hard to muster up the mental energy to give ourselves a sense of direction throughout the day. There fore we find ourselves just "going with the flow". Follow this meditation and it will help you to open your third eye chakra.

  1. Sit in a comfortable position (prefereably the lotus or the half lotus postition) with your back straight. Take a deep breath in then recite the mantra "I am an intuitive and psychic individual".
  2. Notice the thoughts that come and go. Let them drift into the void of nothingness. Refrain from attaching to them, just notice and accept that they are there. Notice the way you feel. Don't become attached to the feeling. Just notice the feeling and accept that it is a part of you.
  3. Bring your attention to your third-eye region where your third-eye chakra is located. Pay attention to the physical sensation around that area. As you take another deep breath in, exhale with a deep long 'O-M-M-M-M' chant. This vibration will stimualte the third-eye region. Exhale til you feel comfortable.
  4. Each time you perform this chant imagine that you are letting go of feelings and thoughts of doubt with every exhale and that you are breathing in positive pranic energy to that region with every inhale.
  5. Repeat this for up to five minutes a day. When your third eye opens there will be no mistaking it. You may consider this experience to be profound but as you progress and become better at doing it you wll realise that it is part of your natural state.

Keeping your third-eye chakra balanced

Once the third-eye chakra has become unblocked and energy is flowing through it freely it is possible for the flow of that energy to become imbalanced. A number of factors can cause this imbalance to take place such as an unhealthy diet, or poor regulation of your emotions. Opening the third-eye chakra does not mean that it will stay open. It must be tended to like a garden and be nurtured and constantly taken care of in order to ensure that an imbalance doesnt occur causing it to close.
