The crown Chakra: What is it?

The crown chakra is the 7th and last major energy centre in the body. This chakra governs our spiritual connection, sense of independence, intuition and out-of-body experiences. It is also associated with the pineal gland which controls our hormones as well as releasing melatonin to help regulate sleep cycles.

A balanced crown chakra can mean that your abilty to utilise cosmic energy to your advantage is optimal. For instance, being in touch with your divine nature can help you to see and understand things that have been and that are to come from a much deeper and spiritual point of view.

Everyday we are presented with opportunities to balance out our crown chakra but it's best to be clear about why you want to do so. Accesing your divine nature while operating in the physical realm can make your journey athrough life alot more insightful and fulfilling.

The crown chakra is an important energy center that can be blocked by a variety of things such as anger, overthinking and doubt. If your crown chakra is blocked it can lead to feeling frustrated, sad, angry and without a sese of purpose. The good news is that you have the power to unblock your crown chakra through self-love, meditation, healthy diet, exercise and being with those you love. Meditation and O-M Chanting to balance the crown Chakra

The crown chakra is the last chakra to be fully activated. This can lead people to feel disconnected from themselves and others, which can cause them to turn their attention outward in search of purpose or meaning. Sensitivity is another symptom that comes with an underactive crown chakra.

Fortunately, there are many ways you can activate your crown chakra! One way is through meditation- when practicing mindfulness, it’s easy for our minds to become distracted by all the thoughts we have floating around. Instead of trying so hard not to think about anything (which often makes us more aware of how much we actually do), try giving yourself a specific focus for your meditation like saying a prayer or repeating a mantra.

  1. Sit in a comfortable position (prefereably the lotus or the half lotus postition) with your back straight. Take a deep breath in then recite the mantra

    "From the unreal, lead me into the real".

    "From darkness, lead me into the light"

    "From death, lead me into everlasting life"

  2. Notice the thoughts that come and go. Let them drift into the void of nothingness. Refrain from attaching to them, just notice and accept that they are there. Notice the way you feel. Don't become attached to the feeling. Just notice the feeling and accept that it is a part of you.
  3. Bring your attention to your crown region where your crown chakra is located. Pay attention to the physical sensation around that area. As you take another deep breath in, exhale with a deep long 'O-M-M-M-M' chant. This vibration will stimualte the crown region. Exhale til you feel comfortable.
  4. Each time you perform this chant imagine that you are letting go of feelings and thoughts of doubt with every exhale and that you are breathing in positive pranic energy to that region with every inhale.
  5. Repeat this for up to five minutes a day. When your third eye opens there will be no mistaking it. You may consider this experience to be profound but as you progress and become better at doing it you wll realise that it is part of your natural state.

Keeping your crown chakra balanced

Once the crown chakra has become unblocked and energy is flowing through it freely it is possible for the flow of that energy to become imbalanced. A number of factors can cause this imbalance to take place such as an unhealthy diet, or poor regulation of your emotions. Opening the crown chakra does not mean that it will stay open. It must be tended to like a garden and be nurtured and constantly taken care of in order to ensure that an imbalance doesnt occur causing it to close.
