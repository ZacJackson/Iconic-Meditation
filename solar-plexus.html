How to open and balance your Solar Plexus chakra

By Zac Jackson

Solar Plexus Chakra Manipura

  • Element: Fire
  • Color: Yellow
  • Location: Just below the center of the rib cage.
  • Attributes: Know to govern how we feel about ourselves, our personal power, and our ability to stand up for ourselves. When this chakra is balanced, you will have self-confidence as well as good social skills.

What is the solar Plexus chakra?

The solar plexus chakra is located at the bottom of your ribcage and it represents our sense of self-confidence, self-esteem, and our ability to take care of ourselves. It can also represent how we respond to change in life. The higher the energy level that this chakra has, the more confident you'll be with dealing with any changes that come up in your life.

The solar plexus chakra is the power center of our body, it connects to our ego and self worth. It is also known as the Manipura chakra and has a deep connection with the stomach organs. When we are feeling confident in who we are, this centre will be strong and radiant.

Opening solar plexus chakra with meditation.

Meditation can be a tricky thing for the mind. There are many styles of meditation, and different concepts pursued by each discipline all to teach us a similar lesson. To live in the present moment, fully understanding all that is going on within it. But what happens when we lose our self-awareness? That's where meditation for the solar plexus steps in to lead you back through your personal journey into yourself─ balancing and unblocking this chakra along the way.

Opening and balancing the solar plexus chakra means allowing yourself to become more confident and courageous in the face of fear. The key to creating a balance is allowing this energy to flow through your naturally and freely without allowing it to cause you to become overconfident and cocky.

  1. Sit in a comfortable position (prefereably the lotus or the half lotus postition) with your back straight. Take a deep breath in then recite the mantra "I am in control of my thought san emotions".
  2. Notice the thoughts that come and go. Let them drift into the void of nothingness. Refrain from attaching to them, just notice and accept that they are there. Notice the way you feel. Don't become attached to the feeling. Just notice the feeling and accept that it is a part of you.
  3. Bring your attention to your the region where your solar plexus is located. Pay attention to the physical sensation around that area. As you take another deep breath in, exhale with a deep long 'R-a-a-a-a-m' chant (make sure to flick the tongue when chanting with the 'R'). This vibration will stimualte the solar plexus. Exhale til you feel comfortable.
  4. Each time you perform this chant imagine that you are letting go of negative energy from that area with every exhale and that you are breathing in pranic energy to that region with every inhale.
  5. Repeat this for up to five minutes a day until you start to notice changes in your ability to expres yourself and be assertive.

Keeping your solar plexus chakra balanced

The Solar Plexus Chakra is the center of physical and emotional power. It rules our individual goals, ambitions, and desires for independence and self-determination. There are many things that can block this chakra including fear, pride and resentment. A person with this blocked will focus on their own needs to a point where they become focused only on themselves. They're afraid of what the world has to offer so they stay in their comfort zone as long as possible before making any social interaction or changing careers; these people may also have a tendency to be self-centered or need excessive attention just from others around them because they lack self worth - it's not uncommon.
