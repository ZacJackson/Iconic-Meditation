Our story

Meditation is an important part of life that many people don't fully understand. Iconic Meditation has taken on the responsibility of spreading awareness to those who need it most in order to improve their quality of living and mental health.

There are many ways to combat the feeling of being unwell. One is by observing your mind, body and emotions and reflecting on what you have done in the past which may be holding you back from achieving a more positive mindset.

Hi, My name is Zac Jackson and I have put this website together in order to raise the awareness of spirituality. I wanted to do something different with my life so I decided to start pursuing what I was passionate about. I have no university degree or any fancy thing to tell you about myself like I'm a spiritual guru of some kind. I am just a normal guy and I strongly believe that meditation is the key to knowing you true Self.

I developed a desire to connect with like minded individuals that are on the spiritual journey of self discovery. Over the past decade I have come to the realisation that what matters most in life is that in order to live it fully we must learn to love and care for one another.
